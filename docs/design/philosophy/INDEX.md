## 3. Key Concepts

### 3.1 信达雅 (Xìn Dá Yǎ)

The three pillars of SAGE design philosophy:

| Principle | Chinese | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| **Faithfulness** | 信 | Accurate, truthful representation |
| **Expressiveness** | 达 | Clear, effective communication |
| **Elegance** | 雅 | Refined, maintainable design |

### 3.2 Design Axioms

Core principles that cannot be compromised:

1. **Simplicity over complexity**
2. **Explicit over implicit**
3. **Composition over inheritance**
4. **Convention over configuration**
