## 3. Design Philosophy (信达雅)

| Principle        | Chinese | Meaning              | Application                             |
|------------------|---------|----------------------|-----------------------------------------|
| **Faithfulness** | 信 (Xin) | Accurate, reliable   | Complete knowledge preservation         |
